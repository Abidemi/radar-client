RaDaR

Welcome to the National Registry for Rare Kidney Diseases (RaDaR). RaDaR is designed to pull together information from patients who have certain rare kidney diseases. The aim is to give a much better understanding of how these illnesses affect people and facilitate research.

This site is where healthcare professionals register patients and enter data. Before registering a patient you will need them to fill in a consent form. To check the patient is eligible for the registry and view a list of diagnoses visit RareRenal.org.
